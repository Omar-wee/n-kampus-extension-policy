Privacy Policy for N-kampus Speed Up Extension

Last updated: 15/05/2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the N-kampus Speed Up extension ("Extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our extension.

Information Collection

The N-kampus Speed Up extension does not collect any personal information from its users. The extension:

  • Does not collect, track, or store any browsing history
  • Does not collect any personal identifiable information
  • Does not transmit any user data to external servers

Local Storage

The extension saves your preferred video playback speed setting locally in your browser's storage. This information:

  • Is stored exclusively on your device
  • Is never transmitted to us or any third parties
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

Website Access

The extension only has access to and functions on perculus4.com. It modifies video playback speed when you hold the space key, but it does not monitor your activity or collect data about your usage of the website.

External Links

The extension contains a "Buy Me a Coffee" link. Clicking this link will take you to an external website (buymeacoffee.com) which has its own privacy policy and data collection practices that are not controlled by us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
